Dell Templates

For creating assets like presentations, stationery, emails, data sheets and other collateral, we have templates available for both the Dell and Dell EMC brands. This site will be updated regularly, so if you don’t see what you need, please reach out to your Corporate Brand Team.

 

See More

Here’s a quick overview of the templates available to you:

PowerPoint Presentations

We have both Dell and Dell EMC PowerPoint templates available, as well as guidance on when to use which template. These templates should be used for both internal and external presentations.

See More

Email signature

Our email signatures use the Arial font and do not include images or banners (unless approved by the Corporate Brand Team). For color and typeface size, please visit your intranet page where you can simply copy a template signature and replace with your information.

See More

Collateral Templates

We maintain a library of self-service templates for producing collateral documents, from data sheets to solution overviews. If you have to create a collateral document, start with the appropriate template, as this will help us to maintain the integrity of the Dell brand. Templates include layout and writing specifications.

See More

Internal Email Newsletters

and Company News

We have templates for internal emails from our business units/organizations, executives and for newsletters. These templates may be used externally, but we recommend consulting your marketing team first to ensure your communication aligns with current campaigns.
